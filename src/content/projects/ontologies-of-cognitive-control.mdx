A two-stage fMRI study paired with a mobile self-control intervention, testing
whether activity in cognitive-control circuits predicts who responds to
treatment. It integrates task-based fMRI, behavioral performance, breathalyzer
and Fitbit streams, and ecological momentary assessment into a single model of
control.

## What it is

I designed and deployed a high-performance computing pipeline (Python, SLURM)
for preprocessing, GLM modeling, and behavioral validation, plus group-level
workflows to evaluate intervention-related changes. I authored the
preregistration and analysis plan.
