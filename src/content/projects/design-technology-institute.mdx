I had the privilege of working under [Constance Swaniker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constance_Swaniker) and Bernice Gavor at DTI and A&A. Constance is an incredible woman — she took her studio and turned it into the driving force behind a national vocational training school, and now she's scaling it across a country where young people struggle to find rigorous training to actually get them work-ready.

My part was mostly technical. I built out the infrastructure to collect, analyze, and securely store survey data from 15,000 students across six campuses, designed the questionnaires, built the pipeline end to end, and worked with faculty and staff so they could keep running it after I left. Threw in a couple lectures too — one on AI, one on personal and professional organization, because apparently I have opinions on that.

Wonderful summer. If you're ever in West Africa, go say hi to DTI and A&A. <EndMark />
