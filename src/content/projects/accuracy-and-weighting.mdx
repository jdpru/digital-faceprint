Seventeen years of YouGov Cooperative Election Study data, compared across
weighting methods. The analysis finds average error in election estimates
exceeds 7% with no improvement over time — a direct challenge to nonprobability
survey claims.

## What it is

Built on a 7,000-line R codebase that harmonizes longitudinal data from fifteen
sources — federal archives, universities, and a private provider via a
negotiated data trade. Presented at AAPOR; code and data archived on OSF.
