The 5-HT2A receptor is the shared doorway for a whole class of psychoactive molecules. What separates them is the fine print: where they sit in the pocket, and how the pocket holds them.

## The analysis

Using SwissDock and PyMol, this project estimates binding energies across a series of ligands and looks closely at the nonpolar interactions that stabilize each pose.

> The interesting variance isn't in whether a molecule binds — it's in what, exactly, is doing the holding.

## Takeaway

Nonpolar contacts account for a surprising share of the stability, which reframes what "fit" means for this pocket. <EndMark />
