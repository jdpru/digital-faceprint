The climate value of a renewable megawatt-hour depends on what it displaces — and the grid displaces at the margin, not the average. Getting that number right changes which projects are worth building.

## The data

A Python scraper pulls roughly five million data points over six years from CAISO; an R simulation then estimates the marginal emissions offset per MWh of renewable generation.

> Average emissions tell you about the grid you have. Marginal emissions tell you about the grid you change.

## What it estimates

A fixed-effects model, with a heterogeneity analysis by hour and season, puts a defensible figure on the offset — and shows how much it swings with timing. <EndMark />
