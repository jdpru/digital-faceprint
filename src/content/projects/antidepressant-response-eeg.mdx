Choosing an antidepressant is still largely trial and error: a drug, a six-week wait, a verdict, and often a restart. If the resting EEG carried a usable signal about who responds to what, that loop could get much shorter.

## The approach

Semi-supervised and recurrent variational autoencoders compress raw EEG into a latent representation, which a light classifier then reads for response. Training pairs a reconstruction loss with a prediction loss so the space stays both faithful and useful.

> The autoencoder is doing the honest work: it has to describe the signal before it is allowed to guess about it.

## Where it lands

Evaluated with ROC-AUC against clinical and combined baselines, the latent features add signal over demographics alone — modest, but in the direction that matters. <EndMark />
